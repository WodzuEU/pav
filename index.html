INFO

Project: The Pavilion

Location: Poznan, Poland

Author: Jakub Chojnacki

Year: 2025

The Pavilion is envisioned as a contemporary space for reflection and assembly, exploring the interplay of light, structure, and natural materials. Its design prioritizes a minimal environmental footprint while creating a powerful, monolithic presence. The structure serves as both a destination and a journey, guiding visitors through a carefully choreographed sequence of open and enclosed spaces that frame views of the surrounding landscape.

ABOUT

Jakub Chojnacki is a designer focused on creating visionary structures that blend aesthetics, functionality, and sustainability. This portfolio showcases a belief in timeless design that respects its context and is built for the future.
